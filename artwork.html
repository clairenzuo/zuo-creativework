Problem: Proper budgeting is a common issue for post-secondary students. It's often their first experience managing personal finances, and students tend to overspend. Current budgeting apps are catered towards working individuals with full-time jobs. They're usually complex and focused on investing and credit score monitoring. This can prove to be a difficult experience for students beginning their budgeting journey.

Solution: iBudget is an efficient and intuitive app. It's a way for a college student to manage their expenses. They can also adjust future spending accordingly.
