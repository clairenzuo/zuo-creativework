PLAIN JANE - ASAP FERG FT. NICKI MINAJ
Still Striving
2017
I photoshopped two separate images of Nicki Minaj and ASAP Ferg together on a Yellow background, and added rip and crinkle textures. I had a lot of fun altering the typography for this poster. I did a lot of stretching of the text and broke its standard boundaries.

BABUSHKA BOI - A$AP ROCKY
2019
I used an image of A$AP that I found interesting. I centered all the color schemes of the poster around the photo. I focused on the orange and black colors using white as a highlighting color. I really liked the vertical text so I played around with that a lot. Being able to read it was still important to me, so I made sure to cut his name off at the right spot so that the gestault effect could fill in the rest.

BORED - BILLIE EILISH
13 Reasons Why
2017
I loved that photo of Billie so I created the poster based off the image, focusing on green. Her goggles looked futuristic so I added a bubble wrap texture instead of the wrinkle texture I usually use. I matched the futuristic looked with digital styled text for the date. Since Bored and Billie both started with B, I thought it would be fun to have them start from the same point.

HIGHEST IN THE ROOM - TRAVIS SCOTT
JACKBOYS
2019
*I don't support Travis anymore* I really liked this song at the time. Especially the outro instrumental felt like I was floating, so I focused on skies, clouds, and emphasized the color blue. I spliced in an image of travis with an astronaut. I made the background color gradient from blue to white to accentuate the feeliing of going upwards.

90210 - TRAVIS SCOTT
Rodeo (Expanded)
2015
*I don't support Travis anymore* One of my favorite Travis Scott songs. Initially it was all black, but I went back and inverted part of the image. I added snippets of lyrics around the page.
